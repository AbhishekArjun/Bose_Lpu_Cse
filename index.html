🛤️ General Career Pathways
A Student can choose two career pathways. One from the corporate jobs, government jobs, and higher studies offered in the scheme, and the second can be taken as a generic elective/open minor basket of research, entrepreneurship, or study abroad.

🏢 Corporate Jobs

Multiple options or sub-pathways designed according to the competency of the students to prepare them for specific sectors/job profiles as per needs of industry.

🏛️ Government Jobs

Courses to prepare students for Civil services, Public Sector Undertakings and jobs in the Government sector.

📚 Higher Studies

This pathway prepares students for competitive examinations such as GATE, UGC-NET, CAT, MAT etc. and helps in their progression in higher studies / research.

💡 Entrepreneurship

Handholding through Startup School and e-Coaches:

  • To set up new ventures
  • Improve and modernize existing businesses
  • Start new venture related to existing business
  • Engage in Social entrepreneurship

🔬 Research

This pathway helps in the progression of students in research in academia as well as industry. Focus on high-quality technical publications and patents.

✈️ Masters Abroad

Prepares the undergraduate to clear entrance examinations such as GRE, GMAT, IELTS, TOEFL, etc. The student is provided with all kinds of counseling help to make a judicious decision of study abroad destination.

🔑 How to Qualify
Three pillars of entry into the Fast Learner Track
1
Merit-Based Selection
Top 10–20% based on Sem-1 CGPA, coding challenges, hackathon & GitHub performance, and a motivational interview.
2
Dynamic Re-Evaluation
Re-assessed each term via Shuffle stages — not a permanent label. You can join or exit based on performance.
3
Voluntary Opt-In
Students choose to join based on hands-on inclination and commitment to intensive project-based learning.

🔄 Shuffle Stages — Re-Evaluation Criteria
Performance-based assessments at key milestones

🔀 1st Shuffle — After Semester 2

  • Coding & Problem Solving Test
  • Tech & Soft Skills Interview
  • Top ~10–20% based on Sem-1 CGPA
  • Performance in Challenges, Hackathon & GitHub
  • Motivational Interview (Hands-On Inclination)

🔀 2nd Shuffle — After Semester 3

  • Coding & Problem Solving Test
  • Tech & Soft Skills Interview
  • Project Evaluation
  • Progress on LeetCode & Open Source Contributions

🔀 3rd Shuffle — After Semester 4

  • Coding & Problem Solving Test
  • Industry Mentor & Industry Mock Interview
  • Project Progress & Cloud Deployment
  • Progress on LeetCode & Open Source

📊 Categorization — After Semester 5

  • Industry Mock Interview
  • LeetCode & Open Source progress
  • Performance over all semesters
  • Categorized: >30 LPA / >10 LPA / 6–10 / 4–6 / Non-Tech

💡 About LPUNEST

The LPUNEST exam offers convenient choices to candidates: Online remotely proctored from Home (recommended based on available resources) or Test centre-based options. It is not just an entrance exam but also serves as the basis for the comprehensive Scholarship framework to significantly lower course fees.
